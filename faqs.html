Course Structure and Content

How will you asses my progress?

Your progress will be tracked through weekly
writing exercises and conversation practice,
with detailed progress reports provided.

What materials do you use for the lessons?

We use a combination of learning materials
dependent on the type of Portuguese:
Brazilian or European, and the level of
learning, from beginner to advanced.

Do you offer homework or additional
practice exercises?

We encourage students to use Duolingo
in-between classes and we offer fun
homework assignments where students
write about their favorite topics
in Portuguese.

Additional Questions:

Do you offer Portuguese conversation groups or cultural workshops?

We offer conversation groups and cultural workshops every week

How long does it typically take to reach fluency in Portuguese?

This depends on your level of skill. For beginners, we estimate 6 - 8 months to achieve basic fluency. Some people might reach fluency faster, while others may take longer. The key is to be consistent, find joy in learning, and stay motivated throughout your journey. As a general guideline, the more hours you put in actively learning and practicing Portuguese, the faster you'll progress.
